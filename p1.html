About Us


Needle is a leading stitching company that offers a wide range of services and products, catering to individuals and businesses. Their core services include custom tailoring of garments, intricate embroidery, clothing alterations, and repair services. They also provide sewing workshops and classes to foster a community of sewing enthusiasts. Needle stocks high-quality stitching supplies and offers custom embroidery and monogramming services. They collaborate with fashion designers and provide corporate branding through embroidery for businesses and sports teams. With a focus on quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction, Needle aims to bring creative visions to life through the art of stitching.
